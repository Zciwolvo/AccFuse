How We Work

To get started with our service, you'll first need to create an account on our website. This account will serve as the foundation for all your banking information and analytics.

Next, you'll choose one of the banks we work with that you have an account in. Thanks to the PSD2 directive, we can securely retrieve all relevant information about your accounts, including transaction history, balance details, and your personal information such as your name and surname.

What is PSD2?

PSD2, or the Payment Services Directive 2, is a European regulation designed to make payments more secure, boost innovation, and help banking services adapt to new technologies. It mandates that banks open their payment services and customer data to third-party providers (like us) in a secure and standardized way. This allows us to access your financial data safely and offer services such as consolidated analytics across multiple bank accounts.

Once we've obtained your account information, our backend processes and analyzes the data. This analyzed data is then presented to you on your personalized dashboard, offering a comprehensive overview of your financial status across all connected accounts.
